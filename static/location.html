Getting There By Public Transport

  • From the North:
    Take the train to Mannheim Hauptbahnhof (main station). From Mannheim Hauptbahnhof, take the S-Bahn line S3 or S4 towards Bruchsal/Karlsruhe. Get off at the "Rot-Malsch" train station.
  • From the South:
    Take the train to Karlsruhe Hauptbahnhof (main station). From Karlsruhe, take the S-Bahn line S3 or S4 towards Mannheim. Get off at the "Rot-Malsch" train station.

The station “Rot-Malsch” is within walking distance (nice path along the forest). Trains will also run at night - so no worries about getting back after the evening event.

Further information on bus and train schedules can be found on the Web site of the Rhein-Neckar public transport association: www.vrn.de

Parking Information

Parking is available at the SAP University. Follow the signs to the designated parking areas upon arrival.
The closest parking facilities to the venue are the car parks P1/P2/P3/ as well as the parking area P5. Parking is free.
