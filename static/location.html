↘ Helping Hands

At reCAP, we're all about collaboration, community, and maybe just a little chaos (the good kind). That's why we're on the hunt for amazing people like you to join us as volunteers! Think of it as being part of a superhero squad—only your cape might be a lanyard.
As a reCAP volunteer, you'll be a key player in making this conference unforgettable. In return, we'll shower you with exclusive perks: access to the event, delicious food and drinks, a heartfelt thank-you gift, and, of course, our undying gratitude (we may even write you a sonnet).

Explore Our Volunteer Opportunities:

Setup & Takedown

Love the smell of freshly printed signs and the thrill of room makeovers? Dive into the behind-the-scenes magic! Help us transform our venue before and after the event. You'll handle simple yet vital tasks like arranging signs, decorating rooms, and preparing the registration area. Pro tip: wear comfy shoes—you're in for some hands-on fun!

Registration

Be the welcoming face of reCAP—the one everyone remembers because you gave them their badge and a smile. You'll make check-in smooth, hand out badges like a pro, verify tickets, and field questions like a reCAP oracle. Bonus points for answering “Where's the restroom?” without missing a beat.

Moderator

Love the spotlight, but only when it's on someone else? This role's for you! Introduce speakers, keep sessions running like clockwork, and manage the flow of Q&A. Basically, you're the glue holding speakers, audience, technicians, and the reCAP team together. Yes, you're that important.

Photography & Video

Got an eye for the perfect shot or a knack for capturing those candid “aha” moments? Join us to document the magic of reCAP. Whether you're wielding a camera or crafting video content, youll help us relive the best moments long after the lights go down.

Join the #reCAP Dream Team!

Ready to make a difference and have a blast? Drop us an email with your name, your volunteer role of choice, and a short note about why you're excited to join us. We're counting the days until we get to welcome you to the team and share all the juicy details!

For any questions, feel free to contact us.
